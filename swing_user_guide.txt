───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE 6 SWING SCAN CRITERIA EXPLAINED
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  C1 — TREND
       Price above 50-SMA AND 50-SMA above 200-SMA
       (Both intermediate and long-term uptrend)

  C2 — RELATIVE STRENGTH
       Stock 50-day return beats QQQ 50-day return
       (Outperforming the index)

  C3 — RECENT MOMENTUM
       Positive 20-day return
       (Move is current, not stale)

  C4 — LIQUIDITY
       Average daily volume ≥ 2 million shares
       (Higher than options watchlist requirement of 1M)

  C5 — TRADEABLE VOLATILITY
       ATR(14) between 1.5% and 5% of price
       (Not too tight to make money, not too chaotic)

  C6 — NO BIG GAPS
       No single-day move > 8% in last 20 days
       (Avoids stocks with earnings-style gap behavior)

  MODERATE: pass 4 of 6 (default)
  STRICT: pass 5 of 6 (use rarely)


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE PULLBACK SETUP — WHAT TO LOOK FOR
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The scanner identifies a TRADEABLE pullback when ALL of these
are true:

  1. Stock in confirmed uptrend
  2. Pullback 3-8% from recent swing high
  3. Pullback has lasted 3-15 trading days
  4. Volume on pullback days < volume on prior advance
  5. Reversal candle printed yesterday
       (Hammer / Engulfing / Strong Bull / Inside Bar)
  6. Today: price still holds above pullback low

Entry: BUY STOP at $0.01 above yesterday's high
Stop:  Below pullback low or 1.5× ATR (whichever closer)
T1:    Entry + 2× risk per share (sell 50% here)
T2:    Entry + 4× risk per share (trail rest with 2× ATR)


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TRADE MANAGEMENT QUICK REFERENCE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHEN PRICE REACHES +1R:
   Move stop to entry price (breakeven)
   Cancel old stop, replace with GTC at entry

WHEN PRICE REACHES +2R:
   SELL 50% of shares at market
   Move stop on remaining 50% to entry + 1R

AFTER +2R (TRAILING):
   Each day: new_trail = today's close - (2 × ATR)
   Stop only moves UP, never down

TIME STOP (DAY 10):
   If trade hasn't reached +1R in 10 days: exit at market

REGIME REVERSAL:
   If QQQ flips 🟢 → 🟠 → 🔴 in any 2-day window:
   Close all open longs immediately
